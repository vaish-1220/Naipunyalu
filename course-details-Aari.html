Aari Work

An Aari work course offers an exciting opportunity to delve into the intricate and timeless embroidery techniques that hold a special place in Indian craftsmanship. Through this course, participants will master the use of specialized needles to create elegant and delicate patterns on fabrics, perfect for enhancing garments, accessories, and decorative pieces. Whether you're a beginner or an artisan seeking to refine your skills, this course combines creativity, precision, and tradition, equipping you to bring your artistic visions to life through the beauty of Aari embroidery.

Zardozi Aari Work

Joy of threads and beads

This style uses metallic threads, beads, and sequins to create opulent designs, often seen in bridal wear and luxurious textiles.

Beaded Aari Work

knitting Life

Incorporating beads of various sizes and colors, this technique adds texture and vibrancy to the embroidery, perfect for enhancing accessories and garments.

Thread Aari Work

Embroidery of Happiness

A classic form of Aari embroidery, it relies solely on colorful threads to craft delicate and intricate patterns, ideal for traditional and contemporary designs alike.
