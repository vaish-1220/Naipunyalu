Courses

At Naipuṇyālu, we offer expert-led courses in traditional Indian art forms, preserving and promoting our rich cultural heritage. From the intricate strokes of Bridal Mehandi to the delicate embroidery of Aari Work and the vibrant patterns of Sanskar Bharati Rangoli, our courses provide hands-on learning with skilled artisans. Join us to master these timeless crafts and keep the artistry alive!
